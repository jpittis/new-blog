Introducing Toxiproxy Haskell

With the release of toxiproxy-haskell on Hackage, I’d like to introduce the Haskell community to failure testing using Toxiproxy so that they can improve the resilience of their network connected applications. Toxiproxy Toxiproxy is a cross platform TCP proxy that lets you simulate latency, timeouts, rejections and other network conditions in your test and development environments.
